# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing! Please follow the steps below to make sure your contribution aligns with the project’s vision and standards.

## Requirements

* Strong understanding of **Domain-Driven Design (DDD)**
* Commitment to **Test-Driven Development (TDD)**
* At least **4 years of experience with Laravel** and its ecosystem

## Contribution Process

1. **Fork the repository** and create your branch.
2. **Write tests first** before implementing your code.
3. **Implement your changes** following coding standards and best practices.
4. **Document your contribution**:
   * Provide an **ERD or diagram** of the changes.
   * Explain how your solution ensures both SQL and document databases work together.
   * Include clear documentation of what was changed, why it matters, and how to use it.
5. **Open a Pull Request** with:
   * Code and tests
   * Documentation and diagrams

Our team will review, run automatic tests, and validate your contribution.
